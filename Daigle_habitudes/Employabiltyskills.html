Important traits for a employee to be successfull

professionalism

Professionalism is the ablility for one to carry him/herself in a mature and respeactable manner with competance. Professionalism is the cornerstone to any real company, without it the people who work for it will fail and the company will go bust.

Problem Solving

problem solving is were a individual or a group has the skill to recognize and fix a problem in time. problem solving is why we are advance as we are today, those who do not problem solve are sure to fail along with the organization or group that follows them.

motivation

Motivation is the state of or condition were team member and individuals execute personal drive to accomplish a given task. The results from this can range from small bonuses and leg-ups for a company all the way to a whole country having a advantage.

punctuality

Punctuality is the personal condition of appearing on a given time when told to. Good Punctuality can make the difference between pass and fail.

Communication

communication is important for a group to succede, solve problems, share new ideas and help one another. A lack of communcation can lead to tension between group members,Errors, Laziness and overall unproductiveness.

Trust

Trust is when peope assign responsibility to other that they normally would not give out and expect that person to keep the responsibility. Lack of trust lead to people attempting to undermine, work-around and avoid doing task that involve various people.
